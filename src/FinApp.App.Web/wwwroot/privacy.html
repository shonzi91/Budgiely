Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

TandemTab ("we", "us") is a personal budgeting app that helps you track spending, budgets and savings — optionally alongside your own bank accounts through Open Banking. This policy explains what personal data we process and why. It is written to comply with the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Who we are

TandemTab is operated by Stoyan Stoyanov, Sofia, Bulgaria. For any privacy question or to exercise your rights, contact us at stoyanov.stoyan.st@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • Account details — the email address and username you register with, or the name, email and profile picture your sign-in provider (Google/Facebook) shares if you sign in that way.
  • Budgeting data you enter — accounts, categories, budgets, expenses, savings and contributions you create in the app.
  • Bank account information — only if you choose to connect a bank. With your explicit consent we access, on a read-only basis, your account details and transaction history through our Open Banking provider (see below). We never initiate payments or move money.

Open Banking

Bank connections are provided by Enable Banking Oy, a regulated Account Information Service Provider (AISP), which acts as the technical and regulated intermediary between TandemTab and your bank. When you link a bank:

  • You are redirected to your bank (via Enable Banking) to authenticate and grant consent yourself.
  • Access is strictly read-only — transactions, balances and account details. No payments can be made.
  • Consent is time-limited (typically up to 90 days, as required by law) and must be renewed. You can revoke it at any time from your bank or by removing the connection in the app.
  • Fetched transactions are shown to you for review; nothing becomes a budgeting entry until you confirm it.

Why we process it (legal bases)

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the budgeting service you sign up for.
  • Your consent — for accessing bank account information, which you can withdraw at any time.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We share it only with service providers who help us run TandemTab:

  • Enable Banking Oy — Open Banking connectivity (only when you link a bank).
  • Google Cloud — hosting and database, in the EU (europe-west1 region).
  • Google / Facebook — only if you choose to sign in with them.

Storage, security and retention

Your data is hosted in the European Union and transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections; access credentials and bank connection references are held server-side and protected. We keep your data for as long as your account exists. If you leave or delete an account it is archived and permanently deleted after 30 days. You can delete your data at any time by removing your accounts.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you may request access to, correction or deletion of your data, restrict or object to processing, request portability, and withdraw consent (including revoking a bank connection) at any time. To exercise any right, email stoyanov.stoyan.st@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Bulgarian Commission for Personal Data Protection (CPDP) or your local authority.

Cookies

We use only the storage strictly necessary to keep you signed in and remember preferences (such as your theme and language). We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
